The BLISS Experience
Refreshingly simple from start to finish. Drive up and be greeted with a smile by one of our friendly staff. Be personally checked in and choose from one of our customized express wash packages, or roll through as a VIP with one of our unlimited express memberships.
State-of-the-art Equipment
Our advanced equipment is energy-efficient, gentle on your car and on the planet. We are Blue Coral Beyond Green Certified and use professional-grade, biodegradeable products for an effective, earth-friendly shine every time.
Reclaimed Water
Water is life. We've integrated NuWave Industries water treatment and reclamation systems, which removes dirt, oil and grease from water, allowing us to reuse over 70% of our water during the car wash process. Water is on our hearts every day and we do whatever we can to keep it flowing.
A Better Shine
Restore your shine! Our energy-efficient dryers and advanced self-cleaning drying wheels ensure your car is thoroughly dry and its surface is polished to a sparkling shine--every time.
A Better Clean, Inside & Out
Customers will find our powerful vacuum stations at every express location. With convenient trash receptacles and racks for your floor mats, you are easily able to remove dirt, debris, and bacteria from the inside of your car.
